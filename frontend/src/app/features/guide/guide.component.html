Guide

How your devices and settings work.

Heating

The thermostat controls your central heating. Use the On/Off toggle to turn the heating system on or off for the whole home.

On — Heating runs to reach the set temperature. The + and − buttons adjust the target (setpoint) in degrees.

Off — Heating is disabled and will not run until you turn it back on. The setpoint is saved for when you switch on again.

Hot water

Hot water controls the supply of heated water to your taps and showers.

On — The hot water cylinder or boiler is heating water so you have hot water when you need it.

Off — Hot water heating is paused. Use this when you are away or want to save energy. Turn it on again before you need hot water.

Motion sensor

The motion sensor detects movement in its range and sends alerts when motion is detected. It does not have an on/off toggle in the app.

When motion is detected, an alert appears in the Alerts section on the home page with the time. Use this to monitor activity (e.g. when someone enters a room or when you are away).

Living room light

The living room light can be turned on or off from the app.

On — The light is on. Use this when you want the room lit without using a physical switch.

Off — The light is off. Turn it back on from the app or at the wall when needed.
