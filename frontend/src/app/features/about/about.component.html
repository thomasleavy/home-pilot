About Home Pilot

Home Pilot is a smart home control app that puts your heating, hot water, lights, and other devices in one place.

What this app is for

Home Pilot lets you control and monitor your smart home devices from a single dashboard. Whether you want to turn the heating on, run the hot water, switch the living room light, or check when motion was last detected, you can do it from here. The app is designed to be straightforward and accessible, so you can manage your home without fuss.

How it is useful

  • One place for everything — See and control your thermostat, hot water, lights, and sensors from the Home screen instead of juggling several apps or remotes.
  • Insights into your usage — The Insights page shows how long your heating and hot water have been on over recent days, so you can spot patterns and use energy more efficiently.
  • Your settings, your account — Your device states and preferences are stored with your account. When you log in on another device or after reinstalling, your setup is still there.
  • Discover and add devices — Use Discover to find compatible devices and add them to your home so they appear on your dashboard.
  • Support when you need it — Use the in-app guide, FAQ, and support options to get help without leaving the app.

Getting started

After signing in, go to the Home screen to see your devices. Use the toggles and controls to turn things on or off or adjust settings. Check Insights to view your heating and hot water history, and use Manage to change your home details, permissions, or connect to other services.
